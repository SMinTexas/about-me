My Story

My full name is Thomas Steven Murray. I am a middle-namer due to a mistake on the birth certificate, where my parents wanted me to be named Steven Thomas Murray but the nurse who filled out the certificate transposed the first two names. So with my dad also being a Thomas, that resulted in some confusion at times later in life when someone would call asking for Thomas Murray ...

  • Date of Birth: 3 June 1967 at Bellaire General Hospital, Bellaire, Texas
  • Grew up in Lake Jackson, Texas
  • Graduated from Brazoswood High School on 21 May 1985
  • Proud graduate of Texas A&M University, Class of 1989
  • Married to Rhonda with two kids: Tyler (age 27) and Danielle (age 13)
  • Member of Kingsland Baptist Church in Katy, Texas
  • Lives in Cinco Ranch, Katy, Texas, for the last 20 years
